Lasagna

My go to Carbs!

This is one of my Favorite foods to make due to the facts its fast and simple to make. This recipe will not only taste good but will also make you wanting to make more every week.

Lets Get Started:

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Choice of ground meat
  • Garlic
  • Basil
  • Red Pepper(if you want it spicy)
  • Onion
  • Mozzarella cheese
  1. Preheat oven to 350F
  2. Cook noodles until soft
  3. In a skillet put your type of meat and seasoning.
  4. Pour in pasta Sauce
  5. Layer Lasagna noodles with meat in between and cheese. Repeat twice then cover with foil and bake for 45mins.
