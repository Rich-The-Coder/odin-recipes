Chicken Parmesan

The Best Healthy Cheat Meal

Chick Parm is the best thing that has ever been created in my opinion. Your gaining everything you need including as a runner this is a go to because you are gaining carbs protein and the most important thing of them all... Flavor!

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Noodles
  • Tomota sauce
  • Italain Seasoning
  • Mozzarella
  • Eggs
  • Bread Crumbs
  1. Preheat oven to 430. Lightly Grease oven Tray
  2. Crack your eggs and place breadcrumbs on seperate bowl
  3. Clean you chicken, then pat dry and place into eggs and breadcrumb bowl.
  4. Place chicken in into medium heat pan and cook till golden brown
  5. Place noodles into pot with hot water once soft it is good to go.
  6. Place chicken into baking tray and ocver with mozzarella cheese
  7. Place baking tray into oven for 10mins and serve food when done.
